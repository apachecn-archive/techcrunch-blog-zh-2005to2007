# 索尼称美国版 PS3 可能会有 80GB

[![ps380here.jpg](img/ee619b5a1be7f7633876b1e03766906e.png)](https://web.archive.org/web/20150708071209/http://old.crunchgear.com/wp-content/uploads/ps380here.jpg "ps380here.jpg")

那又怎样，索尼将于下个月的[开始在南韩销售 80GB 的 PS3。大不了。我们这些在美国的人呢？嗯，索尼刚刚暗示它正在考虑在这里和其他地方销售 80GB 型号。显然，索尼将决定是否在不同的市场逐个推出这款大男孩。因此，我们仍然看到 20GB PS3 在日本销售，而它在这里](https://web.archive.org/web/20150708071209/http://crunchgear.com/2007/05/21/80gb-ps3-hits-south-korea-next-month/)[停产](https://web.archive.org/web/20150708071209/http://crunchgear.com/2007/04/12/20gb-ps3-discontinued-funeral-service-scheduled-for-next-week/)并且[甚至](https://web.archive.org/web/20150708071209/http://crunchgear.com/2007/03/26/microsoft-thinks-its-cool-crashes-ps3-euro-launch/)从未在欧洲销售。

但是额外的 20GB 值得吗？

在这些天的[万亿字节硬盘](https://web.archive.org/web/20150708071209/http://crunchgear.com/2007/04/26/hitatchi-now-shipping-1tb-hard-drive/)，20GB 几乎四舍五入为零在我的书。相反， [Xbox 360 Elite 的](https://web.archive.org/web/20150708071209/http://crunchgear.com/2007/04/30/really-is-the-xbox-360-elite-worthy-of-an-85-rating/)从 20GB 到 120GB 的跳跃是巨大的，如果不是不必要的[昂贵的](https://web.archive.org/web/20150708071209/http://crunchgear.com/2007/04/05/microsoft-tries-to-justify-120gb-xbox-360-hard-drives-180-price/)。一些游戏玩家可能喜欢憎恨索尼(这似乎是当今很酷的事情)，但我讨厌看到它像这样挣扎。那些[杀手级应用](https://web.archive.org/web/20150708071209/http://crunchgear.com/2007/05/21/blame-it-on-the-lack-of-media/)来得太快了。
