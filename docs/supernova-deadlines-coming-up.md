# 超新星截止日期即将到来| TechCrunch

> 原文：<https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.techcrunch.com:80/2006/04/24/supernova-deadlines-coming-up/>

 [](https://web.archive.org/web/20150918041317/http://www.supernova2005.com/) [超新星](https://web.archive.org/web/20150918041317/http://www.supernova2005.com/)，包括 [TechCrunch 新公司展示会](https://web.archive.org/web/20150918041317/http://supernova2006.com/techcrunch.htm)，于 2006 年 6 月 21-23 日在三藩市举行。

如果你想在[新公司展示会](https://web.archive.org/web/20150918041317/http://supernova2006.com/techcrunch.htm)上展示你的公司，公司**申请截止日期是 2006 年 4 月 28 日**(本周五)。如果您想成为十家参展公司之一，请填写[申请表](https://web.archive.org/web/20150918041317/http://www.supernova2006.com/downloads/techcrunch_form.doc)并提交给[techcrunch@supernova2006.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20150918041317/mailto:techcrunch@supernova2006.com)。
