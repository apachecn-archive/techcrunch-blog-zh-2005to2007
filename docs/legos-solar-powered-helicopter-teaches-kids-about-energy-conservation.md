# 乐高的太阳能直升机教孩子们节约能源

[![legoheli.jpg](img/8d580d620b75e4b74f0cc7d0f1a41236.png)](https://web.archive.org/web/20210304161058/https://beta.techcrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2007/04/legoheli.jpg "legoheli.jpg")
