600 美元[的 LG 普拉达](https://web.archive.org/web/20210125150706/http://crunchgear.com/2007/04/18/koreas-lg-prada-has-dmb-tv-europes-doesnt/)是威瑞森无线的[回答](https://web.archive.org/web/20210125150706/http://crunchgear.com/2007/05/23/att-has-iphone-exclusively-for-five-years-verizon-preps-answer/)到 500 美元[的苹果 iPhone](https://web.archive.org/web/20210125150706/http://crunchgear.com/2007/05/21/att-to-use-iphone-to-push-name-change/) …好了，你现在可以不笑了。
