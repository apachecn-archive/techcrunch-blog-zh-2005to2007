# 两周内的两场派对——西雅图和伦敦

过去两周，我们举办了两场 TechCrunch 派对。上周我们一群人在伦敦聚在一起喝了几杯，昨晚我们在 T4 西雅图[的 Conworks 酒吧喝了个痛快。](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/2006/05/25/techcrunch-seattle-party-next-week/)

认识新的人，或者我在网上认识但没有见过面的人，感觉很棒。来自[伦敦活动的图片在这里](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://flickr.com/photos/tags/techcrunchlondon/)，昨晚的[西雅图图片在这里](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://flickr.com/photos/tags/techcrunchseattle/)。如果您想添加您自己的活动图片，请添加“TechCrunchLondon”和“TechCrunchSeattle”作为 flickr 标签。

如果你写了这两个事件中的任何一个，请联系我，我会给这个帖子添加一个链接。昨晚的活动已经有一堆了。Redfin 博客文章[在这里](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://blog.redfin.com/blog/redfin/2006/06/geeks_gone_wild.html)。[西雅图邮报情报](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://blog.seattlepi.nwsource.com/venture/archives/103894.asp)的约翰·库克写了很多参加会议的创业公司。听听斯图尔特·马克斯韦尔和阿里·达尼阿里的播客[这里](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://www.seattlepodcasting.net/?p=98)和[这里](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://alidaniali.backpackit.com/pub/611586)，看看[吉姆·格兰杰](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://www.jimgranger.com/blog.php?article=4)、[大卫·布鲁内尔](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://www.davidbrunelle.com/2006/06/01/techcrunch-seattle-party/)、[迈克·弗莱德根](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://travelstartups.com/2006/06/techcrunch-seattle-travel-20.html)和[瑞安·斯图尔特](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://www.digitalbackcountry.com/index.cfm/2006/6/1/Partying-with-Flash-in-Seattle)对这次活动的看法。更有[这里](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://jayandsilentrob.com/2006/06/01/techcrunch-seattle/)、[这里](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://jayandsilentrob.com/2006/06/01/downsouth-wrap-up/)、[这里](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://bravenewword.typepad.com/brave_new_word/2006/06/techcrunch_part.html)、[这里](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://www.blueace.nl/2006/06/party-like-its-1999-alhoewel/)、[这里](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://collections.lopolis.com/archives/2006/06/01/techcrunch-web-20-party-write-up/)和[这里](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://www.herdrick.com/herdrick/index.html)。

所以，感谢来到伦敦 TechCrunch 的大约 150 人，以及昨晚在西雅图出席的大约 300 人。我还要感谢赞助者，他们支付了所有费用——GNR 报道了伦敦的活动，Redfin、Farecast 和 Triphub 支付了西雅图的活动费用。

我将在 7 月初回到伦敦(希望我们能举办另一场活动)，本月晚些时候回到西雅图参加 [Gnomedex 6](https://web.archive.org/web/20220124122359/http://www.gnomedex.com/) (Gnomedex 5 是我多年来参加过的最好的会议)。
