*   从 1986 年到 1996 年，微软的股票飙升了 100 倍，据估计，到 2000 年，微软已经创造了 10，000 名百万富翁*   "估计已经为 Windows 开发了 25-30 万个应用程序."*   “If Bill drops a thousand dollar bill, it’s not worth his while to pick it up, as he’ll make the same amount in the time it takes him to pick it up.”
