# Twitter 完成了他们的冒险

[![twitter.png](img/5e63ca7315454de4d731e804f23d6f85.png) ](https://web.archive.org/web/20230203002933/http://www.crunchbase.com/company/twitter) [推特](https://web.archive.org/web/20230203002933/http://www.crunchbase.com/company/twitter)已[筹得](https://web.archive.org/web/20230203002933/http://twitter.com/blog/2007/07/taking-bite-out-of-big-apple.html) a 轮融资，[由联合广场创投领投](https://web.archive.org/web/20230203002933/http://www.unionsquareventures.com/2007/07/twitter.html)。这轮融资的规模尚未披露，但其他投资者包括 Charles River Ventures、马克·安德森、迪克·科斯特罗、罗恩·康韦和 Naval Ravikant。

这项服务拥有大约 30 万用户，最近几个月发展迅速。 [Pownce](https://web.archive.org/web/20230203002933/http://www.crunchbase.com/company/pownce) 、 [Kyte](https://web.archive.org/web/20230203002933/http://www.crunchbase.com/company/kyte) 等都是竞争对手。

点击此处查看 Duncan Riley 对创始人比兹·斯通的[采访。](https://web.archive.org/web/20230203002933/http://techcrunch.com/2007/05/30/twitter-back-on-the-straight-and-narrow/)

**更新:**听起来，该公司与投资者就不披露这轮融资的规模或任何其他细节进行了激烈的谈判。没人说话，除了把范围缩小到 100 万到 500 万美元之间。
