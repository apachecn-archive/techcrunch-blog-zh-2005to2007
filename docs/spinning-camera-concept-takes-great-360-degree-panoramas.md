# 旋转相机概念拍摄 360 度全景照片

> 原文：<https://web.archive.org/web/http://techcrunch.com:80/2007/08/10/spinning-camera-concept-takes-great-360-degree-panoramas/>

[![spincam1.jpg](img/391c8f4e5defd26c1cfdd8dae2a12e9f.png)](https://web.archive.org/web/20160512131701/http://old.crunchgear.com/wp-content/uploads/spincam1.jpg "spincam1.jpg")

这个旋转的数码相机标志着[连续第二天](https://web.archive.org/web/20160512131701/http://crunchgear.com/2007/08/09/combo-cdmp3-player-concept-puts-other-daps-to-shame/)我看到了一个聪明、有用的概念。我的整个世界就在我面前崩溃了。它的工作原理就像一个带有内置摄像头的陀螺(我猜是 dreidel)。旋转相机，它可以拍摄该地区的 360 度全景。像往常一样，所有照片都存储在存储卡上。

[![spincam2.jpg](img/354606b9b87ea23bc7aab5f5d01f2e7f.png)](https://web.archive.org/web/20160512131701/http://old.crunchgear.com/wp-content/uploads/spincam2.jpg "spincam2.jpg")
