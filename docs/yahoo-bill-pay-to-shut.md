# 雅虎支付关闭 

> 原文：<https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.techcrunch.com:80/2007/07/06/yahoo-bill-pay-to-shut/>

雅虎[宣布](https://web.archive.org/web/20221003212447/https://bills.secure.yahoo.com/shutdown.html)[雅虎账单支付](https://web.archive.org/web/20221003212447/http://finance.yahoo.com/bp)将在今年九月至十月间关闭。

Yahoo Bill Pay 于 1999 年 9 月推出，并被宣传为给雅虎用户提供“从任何连接到互联网的电脑上安全支付账单”的能力。

这项服务将分两个阶段关闭。9 月 14 日将是使用该服务支付账单的最后一天。在 10 月 31 日之前，以前的交易仍然可以查看，届时该服务将完全关闭。

雅虎 Bill Pay 的关闭是自 5 月份以来雅虎首次宣布关闭服务，当时雅虎宣布关闭 [Webjay](https://web.archive.org/web/20221003212447/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/2007/05/23/webjay-joins-deadpool-yahoo-scores-hat-trick-for-may/) 、[雅虎照片](https://web.archive.org/web/20221003212447/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/2007/05/03/breaking-yahoo-to-announce-closure-of-yahoo-photos-tomorrow/)和[雅虎拍卖](https://web.archive.org/web/20221003212447/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/2007/05/09/yahoo-shutting-down-auctions-second-service-to-deadpool-this-month/)。雅虎 Bill Pay 加入 [TechCrunch 死池](https://web.archive.org/web/20221003212447/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/tag/deadpool)。
