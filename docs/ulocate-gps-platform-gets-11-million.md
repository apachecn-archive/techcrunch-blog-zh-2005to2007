# uLocate GPS 平台获得 1100 万美元| TechCrunch

> 原文：<https://web.archive.org/web/http://techcrunch.com:80/2007/05/16/ulocate-gps-platform-gets-11-million/>

[![ulocatelogo.png](img/c314219dea7c64966b2308798b9346fd.png) ](https://web.archive.org/web/20160325175852/http://ulocate.com/) uLocate 今天上午通过[商业专线](https://web.archive.org/web/20160325175852/http://home.businesswire.com/portal/site/google/index.jsp?ndmViewId=news_view&newsId=20070516005650&newsLang=en)宣布获得 GrandBanks Capital 和 Kodiak Venture Partners 的 1100 万美元投资。格兰班克斯资本公司和科迪亚克风险投资公司。Venrock 的普通合伙人 Mike Tyrrell 也加入了 uLocate 的董事会。

uLocate 的产品作为移动运营商的各种 GPS 定位系统的接口，使开发者能够创建基于位置的应用程序。他们有一些人定位( [Mapquest](https://web.archive.org/web/20160325175852/http://findme.mapquest.com/) 、 [Trackem](https://web.archive.org/web/20160325175852/http://trackem.com/) )、地点搜索( [kmaps](https://web.archive.org/web/20160325175852/http://kmaps.ulocate.com/) )和照片地理标记( [GeoSnapper](https://web.archive.org/web/20160325175852/http://geosnapper.com/) 、 [Where](https://web.archive.org/web/20160325175852/http://www.where.com/) )的实例。

对基于位置的移动应用感兴趣的读者也应该看看 [Loopt](https://web.archive.org/web/20160325175852/http://www.techcrunch.com/2006/09/11/loopt-to-make-mobile-presence-usable/) ，它支持 Boost mobile 的朋友查找服务。
