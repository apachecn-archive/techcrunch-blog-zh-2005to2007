# Rallypoint 提供了一个 Ajax Word 解决方案

 [](https://web.archive.org/web/20201025094105/http://www.rallypointhq.com/) 我喜欢今晚的 ajax office 解决方案。除了 [Jotspot Tracker](https://web.archive.org/web/20201025094105/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/2005/12/08/450/) ，我还一直在玩一款在线 ajax word/wiki 产品 [Rallypoint](https://web.archive.org/web/20201025094105/http://www.rallypointhq.com/) 。

Rallypoint 加入了 [Writely](https://web.archive.org/web/20201025094105/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/2005/08/31/writely-process-words-with-your-browser/) 、 [JotSpot Live](https://web.archive.org/web/20201025094105/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/2005/09/27/jotspot-live-the-perfect-wiki/) 、 [Zoho Writer](https://web.archive.org/web/20201025094105/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/2005/10/02/zoho-writers-ajax-word-processor/) 和其他服务(我将 [Writeboard](https://web.archive.org/web/20201025094105/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/2005/10/02/writeboard-launches-but-needs-feature-upgrades/) 和即将推出的 [Chalk](https://web.archive.org/web/20201025094105/http://www.beta.techcrunch.com/2005/08/05/profile-chalk/) 也归入这一类别——所见即所得维基和可共享的 word 在线版本实际上是同义词)。

Rallypoint 有一些很棒的功能，但是还没有 Writely 那么健壮。该测试帐户只允许 2 MB 的存储空间。虽然他们允许从 word(？)，它们没有导入或导出功能..Rallypoint 确实有很棒的图片和媒体上传(内置播放器)，以及丰富的分享/许可功能。

想了解更多关于 Rallypoint 的信息，请查看 Brian Benzinger 关于该产品的帖子。
